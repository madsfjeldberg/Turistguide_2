Eiffel Tower

A wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France.

Statue of Liberty

A colossal neoclassical sculpture on Liberty Island in New York Harbor in New York City.

Great Wall of China

A series of fortifications made of stone, brick, tamped earth, wood, and other materials.

Machu Picchu

An Incan citadel set high in the Andes Mountains in Peru.

Taj Mahal

An ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the Yamuna river in the Indian city of Agra.
